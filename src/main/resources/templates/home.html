About the Project

This application helps parents track their children's growth from infancy to adulthood. Using modern technology, it provides growth charts, BMI calculations, vaccination reminders, and many other useful features to support child development.

Key Features

Height & Weight Tracking

Update measurements over time and compare with WHO standards.

Vaccination Reminders

Receive alerts for important vaccines at each growth stage.

Nutrition Guidance

Personalized meal recommendations based on age and health conditions.

BMI Calculator

Enter height and weight to check body condition.
